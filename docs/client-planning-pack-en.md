# Ireland Chinese Journal Website Planning Pack

## 1. Purpose

This document supports the Phase 1 demonstration website for Ireland Chinese Journal. The goal of this phase is to show a clear website direction, content structure, and homepage layout that can extend the existing WeChat presence into a more permanent media platform.

The website should not replace the WeChat page. It should support it by giving important stories, guides, videos, events, and cooperation information a more organized place to live.

## 2. Project Positioning

Ireland Chinese Journal can be positioned as a trusted Chinese-language community media platform for Chinese residents, students, families, professionals, visitors, and local businesses in Ireland.

The core value is:

- reliable Chinese-language information about Ireland
- stronger visibility for the Chinese community in Ireland
- practical guides for living, studying, travelling, and settling in Ireland
- a professional cooperation channel for local businesses and community partners

Commercial growth should be presented as a natural extension of the media platform, not as the only purpose of the website.

## 3. Target Audience

- Chinese residents living in Ireland
- Chinese students planning to study or already studying in Ireland
- Chinese families looking for local information and weekend activities
- New arrivals who need practical guides about housing, visa renewal, banking, transport, and community services
- Local Chinese businesses looking for promotion and customer reach
- Community groups, event organizers, and cultural partners

## 6. Content and Feature Ideas for Later Phases

### Local Business Promotion

A dedicated business promotion area can help local restaurants, agencies, shops, education services, travel companies, and professional service providers reach the Chinese-speaking audience in Ireland.

Possible formats:

- sponsored article
- business profile page
- homepage promotion slot
- event sponsorship
- seasonal campaign page
- WeChat plus website bundle

### Local Tour Guide Suggestions and Matching

The platform can later include guide recommendations for visitors, students, families, and business travellers.

Possible matching needs:

- family-friendly weekend trips
- student budget routes
- private Chinese-speaking local guides
- airport pickup and first-week settlement support
- custom routes for visiting relatives
- cultural and food tours

This can become a useful service channel while also creating new content for the website.

### Short-Form Video News Feed

A TikTok-style short video section can increase attention and support advertising opportunities, especially for mobile users.

Recommended framing:

- short community updates
- local event highlights
- one-minute practical guides
- student life clips
- business spotlights
- quick interviews

This should be connected with WeChat, TikTok, Instagram, or Xiaohongshu content rather than treated as a separate editorial workload from the beginning.

## 7. Phase 1 Scope

The current phase should stay simple:

- show the visual direction of the website
- confirm the main sitemap
- demonstrate the homepage content structure
- show how WeChat content can be expanded into web articles, video, guides, and events
- prepare a clear foundation for later advertising and business cooperation

## 8. Suggested Next Steps

1. Confirm the final sitemap and homepage sections.
2. Replace sample articles with real WeChat content or selected past posts.
3. Choose the first 10-15 articles or posts to migrate.
4. Define cooperation packages for local businesses.
5. Decide whether the next phase should focus on content management, advertising slots, or short video feed.
